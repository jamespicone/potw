After Armsmaster's fall from grace during Leviathan's attack on Brockton Bay, Miss Militia was promoted to the captain of the Protectorate ENE. It's been a long time coming - she's a natural leader, and well-respected. Even the criminal underworld takes her seriously when they must work together against more serious threats.

Unfortunately for her, the Slaughterhouse 9 are on their way, and her city is soon to become a battlefield...
