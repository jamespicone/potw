After the Undersiders killed Coil, the Echidna event left the Protectorate in disarray, and Skitter defected, Brockton Bay was in shambles. There was a huge power vacuum, and the Undersiders were poised to exploit it.

So Tattletale did. She'd always wanted the Undersiders to emulate the Yakuza of times past, becoming the authority of the criminal underworld, established so firmly that the Protectorate couldn't possibly root them out.

Nobody else was going to rule Brockton Bay, so she would.
