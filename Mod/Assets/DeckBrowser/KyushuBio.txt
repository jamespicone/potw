It is November of 1999, and the Endbringer Leviathan is attacking the island of Kyushu. It is only the fifth time this new Endbringer has emerged, and the powers of the world are still refining their tactics and approach. Japan's native heroes, the Sentai Elite, are out in force, as are the Triumvirate and a number of other local parahumans.

But something about this attack is different than the last. Leviathan is planning something...
